About Us

Who We are

Jomvu Kuu Muslim School is the perfect and conducive environment to let your child learn, explore and grow in academics and holistic
areas as the cool environment allows for the mind to settle and also for the child to enjoy.
We have highly trained and skilled teachers in both formal education and religious education.
We also pride ourselves in integrating Madrassa Education and Computer studies in our school.
Our institution is full C.B.C integrated and offers digital lessons like YOTO using digital devices like smart TVs and tablets.
Our school has a feeding programme which includes a balanced diet and daily outdoor activities.
We welcome you to come and have a look, We are located just after Jomvu Kuu Hall.
